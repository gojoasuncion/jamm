About me

Hi, I’m James Asuncion, a 17-year-old Grade 12 student from the ICT A strand at Taguig National High School. I’m passionate about technology, games, and anime, and I like to balance my love for learning with interests like drawing and sports. Of course, I also enjoy a little fun break during class now and then. With my curiosity and drive, I’m excited to explore and grow in the world of ICT and beyond!. One of my all-time favorite anime is Dragonball – there's just something about the intense battles and epic transformations that always keeps me hooked. I also love Jujutsu Kaisen for its incredible fight scenes and cool characters like Itadori Yuji and Gojo Satoru. When it comes to my favorite color, it’s definitely navy blue – it’s calm, cool, and just feels right. As for sports, I enjoy playing badminton sepak takraw– they’re fast-paced and so much fun! And of course, my favorite food is adobo. it’s the perfect mix of flavors and always hits the spot.
